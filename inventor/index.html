inventor

Print the things. Change the world.

When you think of new product, you focus on the significance of possibilities it brings. And this device, puts the possibility of creating new products in hands of everyone. It’s not just the device that enables you to do more, it empowers you to do probably the best works in your life.

Think greater. Build bigger.

It’s the one, massive piece of technology that doesn’t limit its users. You’re almost borderless in what you can create with it. Model of Eiffel Tower? Space rocket? It all fits there.

Perfect. Again. And again.

Two 3D—printed instances of the same model will always look and feel identical. You won’t notice any differences — inventor has nanometric nozzle than can shape with an accuracy of microns. And because it was made of hardened metal, it will retain its properties for years.

Always leveled up.

Uneven ground your printer stands on may affect on quality of prints. But with inventor it’s not anymore the case. It uses system called automatic ground leveling. Sensor probes ground in 3 points and then calibrates the device. With the time you just saved a whole new world of possibilities opens up to you.

Beautiful monochromatic display.

And the power to do extraordinary things behind it. You can control your printer and print things without computer. Just put in your SD card.

Proper printer requires proper handling.

While those task may look simple in using, they’re complex in computing. State—of—the—art ATmega processor provides high speed connectivity with your computer, essential for 3D printing. Efficient stepper drivers bring performance and high quality to the printer.

Stays cool.

Set of several thermometers and two fans enable to maintain right temperature of the whole unit. The result? Long hours of uninterrupted printing.

You can do anything in this world, but never forget where you come from.
